Title: Arts and Culture events in November

Discover the world of Southeast Asian art in the regional renaissance city of Singapore with the art and cultural offerings this November.

Happening from 15 November–24 November 2019, this two-week long extravaganza celebrates art and inspiration from all walks of life, with painters, street artists and artisans revealing new perspectives, inspirational insights and untold stories.

As you make your way through our nation’s neighbourhoods and precincts, you’ll encounter work and stories from across countries and culture, with workshops, showcases and celebrations that will illuminate and inspire.

Art aficionados looking to learn more about our city’s arts scene can download the Visit Singapore Travel Guide app, available on App Store and Google Play.

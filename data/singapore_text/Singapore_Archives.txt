Title: Singapore Archives

We spent one week in Singapore on our way from Bangkok to Bali. And what a busy and great week it was! There is just so much to see and do in this city.

Singapore is a very modern, urban and “western” city. You hardly feel that you are in Asia, but at the same time Singapore is a true melting pot of the whole of Asia. Here you can find culture and food from both China, India, Malaysia, Indonesia and Thailand. It is a city that has “everything”; fabulous shopping, beaches close by, top notch restaurants, legendary hotels and an endless number of cafes and bars.

Title: Asian Perspectives — Hello! Singapore Tours

Overview

Asian Perspectives is a series of three online sessions covering Hong Kong, Singapore and Tokyo, Japan.

Specially designed for teenagers these sessions showcase what makes each of these Asian mega-cities unique and special.

Experiences can be adapted to cater for younger children or made more in-depth for adults.

Our guides will enthusiastically introduce a variety of subjects including culture, food, city planning, sports, history, trends, architecture, geography and transportation.

Sessions are designed to expand the participants’ perspectives on the world and to spark an interest to see and learn more.
